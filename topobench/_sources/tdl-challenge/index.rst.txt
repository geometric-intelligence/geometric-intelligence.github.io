TDL Challenge
=============

The **Topological Deep Learning (TDL) Challenge** is an annual community
event hosted at the `Topology, Algebra, and Geometry in Data Science
(TAG-DS) Conference <https://www.tagds.com/>`__. Each edition rallies the
community around a focused theme — from architectures and topological
liftings to datasets, infrastructure, and benchmarking — and ships its
contributions directly into **TopoBench**, the open-source platform for
developing and benchmarking TDL models.

Use the links below to access each edition.

Editions
--------

🟢 **2026 — Bridging the Gap** *(current)*
   Uniting the GNN and TDL communities through a shared benchmarking
   ecosystem. Two tracks (GNNs and TNNs), evaluated on synthetic
   GraphUniverse settings via TopoBench.

   👉 :doc:`../tdl-challenge-2026/index`

📦 **2025 — Expanding the Data Landscape** *(archived)*
   Broadening TDL benchmarks with new datasets, large-scale data
   infrastructure, and pioneering benchmark tasks. Hosted at the first
   TAG-DS Conference.

   👉 :doc:`../tdl-challenge-2025/index`

.. note::
   Future editions of the TDL Challenge will be listed on this page as
   they are announced.
